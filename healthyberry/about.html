Our Vision

Yes, you! When Healthy Berry was founded in 2021, our goals as a business was to show love to the natives of Gainesville, Florida. Recent graduates of the University of Florida ourselves, we knew the struggles of maintaining a nutritious diet on a small budget. Healthy Berry was the answer, aiming to increase the accessibility to health drinks to anyone wanting to take the journey. Partnering with local farmers, we entrust all business here supports our community.

Our Values

Provide healthy meal alternatives at affordable costs.

Ensure ingredients are organic & ethically obtained.

Partner with local farmers to support our community.

Maintain transparency with our customers.

Our Future

Transparency and honest connection with our customers is important, which is why we want to keep you updated with our plans for Healthy Berry in the future. We are always listening for suggestions.

Short-Term Goals

  • Developing a mobile app with support.
  • Adding more cuisine options to drinks menus.
  • Online nutritional programs that connects with our smoothies.

Long-Term Goals

  • Expanding locations throughout Florida
  • Partnership with an environmentalist charity.
  • Local Florida farmer's resource page.
