About Me


Hey, my name is Christie! I’m a Graphic Designer and Web Developer that loves finding solutions for people’s problems. I’m a recent graduate of Southern New Hampshire University where I received a Bachelor of Arts in Graphic Design and Media Arts, with a minor in Art History. I’m passionate about my work and find my strength in empathy, always keeping in mind both the user’s and client’s needs.

I have experience in a broad range of areas, including web design, UX/UI design, and graphic design. I’m comfortable coding websites with HTML5, CSS3, and frameworks such as Bootstrap 3, 4, and 5. With my work I blend design with practicality, aiming to provide deliverables that are functional yet still evoke personality.
