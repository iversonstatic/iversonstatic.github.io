Simply Safe

Simply Safe was a project that revolved around designing a mock up for a fictional privacy-focused app. This included researching other applications, demographics, and optimizing UX through sitemapping and wireframes.

DELIVERABLES: UI App Prototype Mock-up, Logo, Brand Design
KEY SKILLS USED: UI/UX Design, Brand Design
TOOLS USED: Adobe XD, Adobe Illustrator
Functions & Roles

Functions
1. Provides overview of user's privacy settings on phone and where they can limit them without disrupting apps.
2. Scans user's information to find out if their data has been breached or is listed online.
3. Blocks unnecessary trackers from apps in the background.
4. Allows users to set up profiles to protect family members.

Roles
1. Privacy Novice - User who is just recently interested in their data and privacy and needs guidance on where to go.
2. Vunerable Senior - A senior citizen who recently has been getting a lot of phishing scams in their emails but doesn't understand technology much at all.
3. Concerned Parent - A parent who wants to prevent their child leaking their information on both of their phones.
4. Witness Protection - A victim who has recently been placed under protection for fear of their abuser finding where they live again.

Branding & Logo Design

The logo was designed with a minimal symbol approach, using a blue color scheme to help draw associations to the tech industry and professionalism.

App Design

For the UI project, I first drew out a site map to help optimize functionality. I chose button placements that were easily accessible and divided the pathway up into a simple three-branch path. At any moment, a user could back out and return to the start of the app and receive an overview of their privacy reports.
