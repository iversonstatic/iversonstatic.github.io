Where the food is always Fun, Fresh, and Filled with Love!

Located nearby the Las Vegas Strip in Nevada...

We at Pasta Amore are welcoming you to join us. We want to provide you and your loved ones with a warm atmosphere for your family where we can share the traditional cuisines of Italy with a modern twist. Here you will be able to choose anywhere from Great-Grandma's authentic recipes to the most popular meals of modern Italy. No matter what you choose, you can rest assure that our food will always be fun, fresh and filled with love.

There is a little slice of history that comes with Pasta Amore. Originally founded by my grandparents Matteo and Giana back in the last 1950's, the restaurant is covered in vintage photographs capturing the era. However, much like the renovations we've done over the past decades, we've made changes to meals of the past to keep our culinary culture growing. We hope you decide to join the family!
